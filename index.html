Frequently Asked Questions :

Do I need any prior experience to join CodeNest?

No experience is needed at all. CodeNest starts from absolute zero assuming you have never written a single line of code. All you need is a laptop, internet connection, and the commitment to learn every day.

How many hours per day do I need to study?

We recommend 2 to 4 hours per day for steady progress. With this pace most students complete the full roadmap and become job-ready within 12 to 14 months. More hours means faster progress but consistency matters more than quantity.

Will I get a certificate after completing the course?

Yes, Pro and Enterprise plan students receive a verified certificate of completion. However we always remind our students that employers care far more about your GitHub portfolio and deployed projects than any certificate.

Can I switch between plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade mid-month you will only be charged the difference. Your progress and projects are always saved regardless of plan changes.

Is CodeNest suitable for university students?

Absolutely. CodeNest is particularly popular among Computer Science students who want practical skills to complement their academic knowledge. Many of our students study at CECOS, FAST, NUST, and other Pakistani universities.
